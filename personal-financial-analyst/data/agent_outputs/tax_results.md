## 3. Investment Tax Strategies

### Tax-Loss Harvesting
- Sell investment at loss → offset capital gains (or up to $3,000 ordinary income)
- Immediately repurchase a similar (not identical) security
- **Example:** $3,000 loss → $660–$720 tax savings
- **Rule:** Avoid wash sale (don't repurchase identical security within 30 days)

### Long-Term vs. Short-Term Capital Gains (2025)
| Holding Period | Tax Rate |
|---------------|----------|
| < 1 year (short-term) | 22–37% (ordinary income) |
| ≥ 1 year, income ≤ $47,025 | 0% |
| ≥ 1 year, income $47,025–$518,900 | 15% |
| ≥ 1 year, income > $518,900 | 20% |

**Strategy:** Hold investments 12+ months; realize gains in lower-income years

### Qualified Dividends
- Taxed at long-term capital gains rates (vs. ordinary income)
- Hold dividend stocks 60+ days around ex-dividend date
- **Savings example:** $10,000 qualified vs. non-qualified dividends at 24% = $900 savings
