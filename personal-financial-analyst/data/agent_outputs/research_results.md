## Quick Wins: Top 5 Easiest Switches

### 1. Cell Phone Plan → MVNO (Mint Mobile, Visible)
- **Monthly Savings:** $30–50 | **Annual:** $360–600
- Switch from major carrier to MVNO; same network coverage

### 2. Streaming Video → Ad-Supported Tiers
- **Monthly Savings:** $15–25 | **Annual:** $180–300
- Netflix Standard ($6.99) + Disney Bundle ($14.99) vs. $40+/mo

### 3. Software → Free Alternatives (Google Docs/Sheets, Canva)
- **Monthly Savings:** $40–60 | **Annual:** $480–720
- Replace Microsoft 365 + Adobe with free/cheaper tools

### 4. News Subscriptions → Apple News+ ($9.99/mo)
- **Monthly Savings:** $25–40 | **Annual:** $300–480
- One subscription covers 300+ publications

### 5. VPN → Cheaper Providers (Proton VPN Plus, Mullvad)
- **Monthly Savings:** $5–7 | **Annual:** $60–84
- Same functionality at $5–6/mo vs $12/mo
